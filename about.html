About StemBuddy

StemBuddy started with the goal to make a one stop calculator hub for several different stem fields. As I take my courses in college I realized that there are so many different calculator sites out there that only calculate one thing. So I decided to make a site that does as many as I can get my hands on.


I will continue to update this page as long as I have time. If you dont see a calculator that you need, contact me and I will get it up ASAP.
